I AM YUGE WANG

DESIGNING RULES. SHAPING SPACES. TUNING SYSTEMS.

I am an Interactive Arts & Technology student specializing in game design, systems thinking, and interactive world-building.

My primary focus is designing and refining core gameplay mechanics — the rules, loops, and decision structures that define how players think and act within a game.

I enjoy analyzing player motivations, shaping decision-making spaces, and tuning systems so every choice feels intentional and impactful. I approach games as dynamic ecosystems where mechanics, numbers, and narrative meaning work together.

My goal is to create experiences that feel coherent, intuitive, and deeply rewarding for players.
